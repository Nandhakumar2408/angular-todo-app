WHAT IS THE REASON ABD IS FAMOUS ALL OVER THE WORLD?

He holds the record for the fastest ODI fifty (16 balls), fastest ODI century (31 balls), and fastest ODI 150 (62 balls). De Villiers captained South Africa in all three formats, although after a series of injuries, he stepped down from the Test captaincy.
